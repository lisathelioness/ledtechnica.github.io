Closing Apps on iOS 7

2 years ago

Of course you could not wait and upgraded your iPhone to iOS 7. In addition to figuring out which of your apps do not work on the new iOS, you also can not figure what to do for closing apps on your iPhone?

Well here is a quick tip on closing apps on your iPhone using the new iOS7 interface:

  • Double click on home button to start the task manager screen
  • Scroll right or left to find the application you want
  • Push the app window upwards for closing the app
